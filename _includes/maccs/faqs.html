Frequently Asked Questions

What is MACCs?

MACCs (pronounced "Max") is a credit card reference app to encourage healthy spending habits and help maximize your rewards.

Why MACCs?

The parents at Koala Health have various credit cards with some rewards as high as 5% on rotational categories that change every 3 months. This is hard to track. So, we decided to make a private, auto-updating reference. No more looking up the new rewards from multiple sources. No giving up on matching rewards to purchases. Just focus on determining your "wants" vs "needs!"

What iPhone models are recommended for MACCs?

The recommended iPhones are iPhone 6S, iPhone 6S Plus, iPhone 7, iPhone 7 Plus, iPhone 8, iPhone 8 Plus, iPhone X, iPhone XS, iPhone XS Max, iPhone XR, iPhone 11, iPhone 11 Pro, iPhone 11 Pro Max, iPhone 12, iPhone 12 Pro, iPhone 12 Pro Max.. MACCs has been tested for all Apple supported iPhones running the latest version of iOS 14 (last updated: 03/13/2021). iPhone SE and iPhones with similar screen resolutions are not recommended. Any older iPhone generations and iPhones not running iOS 14 are also not recommended.

How does Koala Health make money?

We have special affiliates and receive gift donations. We have no interest in selling your identifiable, personal data. We create products/apps that are family-friendly and privacy is an important part of this mission. We hope you will trust us at our word, but we always recommend trusting after verifying over time! We look forward to building mutual trust over time.
